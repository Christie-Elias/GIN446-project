Internet Statistics in the Middle East & Lebanon

Overview

Over the past 20 years, the Middle East has seen a notable increase in internet usage, fueled by advancements in infrastructure, rising smartphone prices, and a more general digital revolution.
Despite its political and infrastructure problems, Lebanon's population is becoming more connected, especially with respect to mobile internet access.

Internet Usage Statistics

By 2029, it is anticipated that mobile data traffic in the Middle East and North Africa will reach 28.34 exabytes (EB) per month. 10.36 EB was the average monthly mobile data traffic in 2024.{11}

Interpretation

According to the report, Lebanon's internet usage rate is higher than the regional average. However, its broadband speeds remain below global standards, indicating infrastructure constraints.
Despite this, a lot of people use mobile internet, hence mobile-first approaches are very important for web development in Lebanon.
